PLT, Gauche, MIT, Gambit, Chicken, Bigloo, Kawa, SISC, Chez support both #; and #| ... |#.

Chibi supports only #;.  (Fix that, willya, Alex?)

SCM has an incompatible meaning of #;.


I recommend including support for both #; and #|...|#.
