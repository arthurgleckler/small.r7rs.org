Testing for the acceptance of `\|' in string literals:

Accepted as equivalent to |: Gauche, MIT, Gambit, Chicken, Bigloo, Kawa, Chibi, SCM, Larceny, STklos, Shoe, Dream, BDC, Rep, UMB, VX, Oaklisp, Llava, Sizzle, FemtoLisp, Dfsch

Not equivalent: Schemik

Lexical syntax error: Racket, Scheme48/scsh, Guile, Vicare, Ypsilon, Mosh, NexJ, KSi, SigScheme, Scheme 9, Scheme 7, SXM, Spark, Inlab, Owl Lisp

Lexer gets confused: Chez, IronScheme

Could not figure out how to test: TinyScheme
