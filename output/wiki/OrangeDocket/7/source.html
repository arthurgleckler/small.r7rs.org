This is an early draft of proposals for the Orange Edition (numbers) of R7RS-large. For other dockets, see [wiki:WG2Dockets].

Multidimensional generic arrays:  [http://srfi.schemers.org/srfi-122/srfi-122.html SRFI 122].

Integer sets and bit vectors:  [http://wiki.call-cc.org/eggref/4/iset IntegerSetsShinn] supplemented with IntegerSetsCowan.

Enumerations with sets and maps:  EnumsCowan

Extended rational numbers:  ExtendedRationalsCowan (based on withdrawn [http://srfi.schemers.org/srfi-73/srfi-73.html SRFI 73]).

Numeric vectors and/or numeric bytevectors:  NumericVectorsCowan and/or BytevectorsCowan.

C-style structs:  [https://github.com/TaylanUB/scheme-bytestructures ByteStructuresTaylanub].

Descriptive statistics:  TallyCowan.

Flonums and compnums:  FlonumsCowan.

Fixnums:  [http://www.r6rs.org/final/html/r6rs-lib/r6rs-lib-Z-H-12.html#node_sec_11.2 R6RS], only without preposterous mustard.

String to bytevector conversion:  StringBytevectorConversionCowan

Integers as bit vectors:  BitwiseCowan (refers to other proposals as well).

Random numbers: [http://srfi.schemers.org/srfi-27/srfi-27.html SRFI 27] plus AdvancedRandomGauche.

Prime numbers:  PrimesGauche.
