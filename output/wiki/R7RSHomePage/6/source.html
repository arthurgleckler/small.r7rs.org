This is the home page for R7RS, the Revised‚Å∑ Report on the Algorithmic Language Scheme.  This version of Scheme has been divided into a small language, suitable for educators, researchers, and users of embedded languages; and a large language focused on the practical needs of mainstream software development.

The report on the small language was finalized on July 6, 2013.  It is available [http://trac.sacrideo.us/wg/raw-attachment/wiki/WikiStart/r7rs.pdf in PDF format] and as [http://trac.sacrideo.us/wg/raw-attachment/wiki/WikiStart/r7rs-small-spec.zip LaTeX source code].  There are [wiki:R7RSSmallErrata errata].
