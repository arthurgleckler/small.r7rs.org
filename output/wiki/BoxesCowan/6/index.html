Boxes

This is a trivial module, providing objects with a single mutable state. Several Schemes have something like it, sometimes called cells. Boxes are a disjoint type.

Procedures

  • box: constructor
  • box?: predicate
  • unbox: accessor
  • set-box!: mutator
